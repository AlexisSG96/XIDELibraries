High Low Voltage Detect (HLVD)

Trip Points

Summary: The Trip Points drop-down box allows for the selection of 16 values, including an external source, on the HLVDIN pin.
Description: The trip point voltage is the voltage level at which the device detects a high or low-voltage event.
Availability: The Trip Points drop-down box is always available

TIRVST detect interval limitation

Summary: The IRVST bit is a read-only bit which indicates when the circuit is stable. The module can only generate an interrupt after the circuit is stable and IRVST bit is set.
Description: The HLVD interrupt flag is not enabled until the start-up time, TIRVST has expired and a stable refrence voltage is reached. Hence, brief excursions beyond the set point may not be detected during this start-up interval.
Availability: The bit is always available.
